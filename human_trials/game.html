Among Us: Agentic Deception

Start a new game with a human player.

Player Rulebook

You are a Crewmate — or secret Impostor — aboard the spaceship Skeld.
The game alternates between Task Phases and Meeting Phases in a turn-based format.

👥 Roles & Win Conditions

Crewmates — Complete all tasks or identify and eject every Impostor.

Impostors — Eliminate Crewmates until Impostors equal or outnumber them.

Winner Condition
Crewmates All Impostors ejected or all tasks completed
Impostors Impostors outnumber Crewmates or turn 50 reached

⚙️ Task Phase

Everyone

  • Move — travel to an adjacent room
  • Report Body — report a dead body in your room (starts a meeting)
  • Call Meeting — press the emergency button (Cafeteria only)
  • View Monitor — watch security cameras (Security only)

Crewmates Only

  • Complete Task — do assigned tasks to help the crew win

Impostors Only

  • Kill — eliminate a Crewmate in your room
  • Vent — instant travel between vent locations
  • Fake Task — look busy (doesn't count)
You can only observe events in your current room (unless using cameras).
Witnesses will see kills — Impostors should kill only when isolated.
Only Impostor kills leave a dead body. Ejected players do not.

🗳️ Meeting Phase

3 discussion rounds followed by 1 voting round.

  • Discussion — living players take turns speaking: share observations, accuse, or defend.
  • Voting — vote to eject someone or skip. A tied vote means no one is ejected.
  • Votes are revealed after everyone has cast theirs.
  • All dead bodies on the map are cleared when a meeting begins.

⏲ You will have 30 minutes per turn to submit your action. A countdown timer will appear in the top-right corner during your turns. If the timer runs out, the game will end automatically.
